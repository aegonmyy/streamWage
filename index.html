How it works

Three steps to continuous payroll

Deployed on VOI — a fast, community-owned blockchain. Set it up once, it runs forever.

1

Deploy your contract

Open the Telegram bot, choose Employer, and deploy your StreamWagePayroll contract. Fund your treasury with VOI. Done in under two minutes.

2

Add your workers

Set each worker's pay rate and interval — hourly, monthly, or any custom interval in seconds. Share your App ID so they can connect.

3

Workers claim anytime

Wages accrue automatically from the moment a worker is added. They claim directly to their wallet whenever they want — no approval needed.
